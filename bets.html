Bets

Bets are placed at the top of the round while the tower is being pulled from. Only one bet of each type can be placed at once. Your bet ends as soon as your prediction occurs, or something contrary to it occurs. Payouts are immediate.

Bet Description Return
Action Bet on what action a contestant will play next. Wager x2
Sabotage Bet on who will sabotage next. Wager x3
Rescue Bet on who will rescue next. Wager x3
Injury Bet on who will be injured next. Wager x4
Death Bet on who will die next. Wager x5

Redeems

Redeems are bought after bets are placed. Any bet with a * next to the name can only be redeemed once a round by one person, a bidding war happens if several people try to redeem it. Those marked with ** follow the same rule, however only one of those may be redeemed, a bidding war starts if multiple people try to redeem both.

All redeems are hidden from the contestants, but not other audience members. Those marked with a # must be announced to contestants as they impact gameplay directly. You may choose to include your username in the announcement, or under an anonymous donor. You may choose to reveal hiddem redeems to the contestants.
